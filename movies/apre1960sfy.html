The Day the Earth Stood Still (1951)

The Day the Earth Stood Still is a black-and-white science fiction film starring Michael Rennie, Patricia Neal, Billy Gray, Hugh Marlowe, and Sam Jaffe. In the film, a humanoid alien visitor named Klaatu comes to Earth, accompanied by a powerful eight-foot tall robot, Gort, to deliver an important message that will affect the entire human race.
