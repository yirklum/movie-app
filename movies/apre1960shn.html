Superman and the Mole Men (1951)

Superman and the Mole Men, starring George Reeves as Superman and Phyllis Coates as Lois Lane, is the first theatrical feature film based on any DC Comics character. The storyline concerns reporters Clark Kent and Lois Lane arriving in a small town to witness the drilling of the world's deepest oil well. The drill, however, has penetrated the underground home of a race of small, bald humanoids.
