The Producers (1967)

The Producers is a satirical comedy film written and directed by Mel Brooks and starring Zero Mostel, Gene Wilder, Dick Shawn, and Kenneth Mars. In the film, producers Max Bialystock and Leo Bloom make money by producing a sure-fire flop called 'Springtime for Hitler: A Gay Romp with Adolf and Eva at Berchtesgaden.'
