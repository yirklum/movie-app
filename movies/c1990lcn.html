Zoolander (2001)

Zoolander is a comedy starring Ben Stiller, Owen Wilson, Christine Taylor, and Will Ferrell. The self-absorbed and dim-witted but good-natured Derek Zoolander is ousted as the top male fashion model by the rising star Hansel. Derek announces his retirement from modeling, but is soon pulled back into the fashion world.
