Don't Eat the Pictures: Sesame Street at the Metropolitan Museum of Art is a one-hour Sesame Street special. The story has the Sesame Street characters getting locked in the American Metropolitan Museum of Art overnight as they embark on an adventure to find their friend Big Bird, who has gotten lost finding Snuffy. They must stay there until the morning while avoiding a security guard. During the movie, Big Bird and Snuffy wander the Egyptian exhibit and encounter an Egyptian prince named Sahu and his cat who have been cursed to remain on Earth. The special also features Cookie Monster, Telly, Bert and Ernie, The Count, Grover, and Oscar the Grouch.
