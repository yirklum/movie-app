Sleeping Beaty (1959)

Sleeping Beauty is an animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney based on The Sleeping Beauty by Charles Perrault. When the young princess Aurora is cursed at birth by the evil fairy Maleficent, the baby is taken by a trio of good fairies who raise the girl themselves, hoping to avoid the spell's fulfillment. Nevertheless, at the age of 16, the beautiful Aurora falls into a deep sleep that can only be awakened by a kiss from her betrothed, Prince Phillip.
