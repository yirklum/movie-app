Davy Crockett: King of the Wild Frontier (1955)

Davy Crockett, King of the Wild Frontier is a live-action Walt Disney adventure film made up of the first three stories from the Disney television miniseries Davy Crockett. Fess Parker plays Davy Crockett, Tennessee-born frontiersman, while Buddy Ebsen co-stars as Davy's sidekick George Russell. The film is divided into three long episodes. Part one shows Crockett fighting on the frontier, part two finds Davy winning a seat in the Nashville legislature, and part three concludes at the Alamo.
