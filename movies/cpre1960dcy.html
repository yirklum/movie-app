Arsenic and Old Lace (1944)

Arsenic and Old Lace is a dark comedy film directed by Frank Capra, starring Cary Grant. The plot revolves around the Brewster family of Brooklyn, New York, descended from the Mayflower and composed of illustrious forebears whose portraits line the walls of the ancestral home. Currently, the Brewster clan is composed of insane murderers.
