Seven Samurai (1954)

Seven Samurai (Shichinin no Samurai) is a Japanese epic samurai drama directed by Akira Kurosawa. The story takes place in 1586 during the Sengoku Period of Japanese history. It follows the story of a village of farmers that hire seven ronin (masterless samurai) to combat bandits who will return after the harvest to steal their crops.
