King Kong (1933)

King Kong is a pre-Code monster adventure starring Fay Wray, Bruce Cabot and Robert Armstrong. The film tells of a huge, ape-like creature dubbed Kong who attempts to possess a beautiful young woman (Wray). King Kong is especially noted for its stop-motion animation.
