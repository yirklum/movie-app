Frankenstein (1931)

Frankenstein is a pre-Code horror monster film adapted from the play by Peggy Webling (which in turn is based on the novel by Mary Shelley), about a scientist and his assistant who dig up corpses to build a man animated by electricity, but his assistant accidentally gives the creature an abnormal, murderer's brain. The resultant monster is portrayed by Boris Karloff in the film.
