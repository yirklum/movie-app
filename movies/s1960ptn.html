Three Days of the Condor (1975)

Three Days of the Condor is a political thriller starring Robert Redford, Faye Dunaway, Cliff Robertson, and Max von Sydow. Set mainly in New York City and Washington, D.C., the film is about a bookish CIA researcher (Redford) who comes back from lunch, discovers all his co-workers murdered, and tries to outwit those responsible until he figures out whom he can really trust.
