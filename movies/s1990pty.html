Munich (2005)

Munich is an historical drama directed by Steven Spielberg and featuring Eric Bana, Daniel Craig, Ciaran Hinds, Geoffrey Rush and Mathieu Kassovitz. At the 1972 Munich Olympics, the Palestinian terrorist group Black September kills eleven members of the Israeli Olympic team. Avner Kaufman (Bana), a Mossad agent of German-Jewish descent, is chosen to lead a mission to assassinate 11 Palestinians allegedly involved in the massacre. Avner's team includes four Jewish volunteers from around the world: South African driver Steve, Belgian toy-maker and explosives expert Robert, former Israeli soldier and "cleaner" Carl, and Danish document forger Hans.
